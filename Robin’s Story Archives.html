MYSTERY BOX STORYBOARD

“Frames of Imagination — Tell Your Story!”** Think you can turn random objects into a creative story? Join us for “STORYBOARDING SHOWDOWN”, a fun challenge where imagination meets unpredictability! Pick *any three items* from our Mystery Box (like a key, leaf, headphone, candle, etc.) and build a complete story based ONLY on those objects. Create a storyboard then recreate it on sticky notes with short descriptions. Show your storytelling skill, frame by frame! This event is all about *visual storytelling* , *creativity*, and how well you turn simple items into meaningful sequences.

Event Details

  • Team Size:SOLO
  • Objective:Participants must: Pick any 3 items from the Mystery Box Create the storyboard on sticky notes Add a 2-line description* for every frame The story can be funny, emotional, mysterious, or fantasy — as long as the 3 items play an important role.
  • Judging: Your storyboard will be judged on:Creativity & Originality,Clear Story Flow,Visual Expression (frames, drawings, sequence),Effective 2-line descriptions,Neatness & Presentation,How well the 3 items were used in the story
  • Venue:To be announced
  • Time: 3:00 PM – 03:30 PM
